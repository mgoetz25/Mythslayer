MYTHSLAYER






How to Play

Athens is under attack by a horde of monsters summoned by the dreadful Typhon! Defend the city as a hero, battling against old enemies.

You have 180 seconds to defeat as many monsters as you can. they will progressively increase in difficulty as you slay more. To attack them, you will need to answer questions in the category you select. Type the answer in the provided box, and if it is correct, you will be given another question, and you will inflict damage upon your foe. For questions that require a number to answer, type the number key rather than the spelling of the number. Use all lowercase letters. For vocabulary questions, give the first and second principle parts on verbs unless stated otherwise. For nouns, give the nominative and genitive. In both cases, use a comma and space to separate the two. On adjectives, simply type the first word in the dictionary entry, which is usually the masculine form.

Do not fail Athens!

Choose your character!

Hover your mouse over a character to read their description.
